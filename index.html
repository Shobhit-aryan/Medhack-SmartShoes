Smart Shoes

A smart device for gait disease recognition, prognosis and fall detection and prevention

Prototype of Footwear

Sensors at the back are used to detect the pressure, velocity , accleration, stride length
IR sensor at front is used to detect any obstacle
Charging point is given at side

FlowChart of Implementation

1st

Download and Register

Wear the shoe and download our app. Register yourself as new user and fill all the necessary details and shoe id.

2nd

Data generation

Now start walking. All the sensors will now start working and will generate data required for gait analysis and fall prevention.

3rd

Transfering data to cloud

The data generated from sensors will be sent to our cloud server through a gsm chip embedded in the shoe.

4th

Machine Learning Algorithms

Our highly efficient machine learning algorithms will help to predict following things.

5th

Degree of gaits

It will check the degree of gaits as low, medium, high risk. If gaits is present,it will suggest required medications and tips.

6th

Routine Check-ups

On a regular basis, it will monitor whether the medications are helping or not. All the medical data will be stored in the cloud and a medical report can be downloaded.

7th

obstacle detection

Elderly people are more visually impaired. So, the infrared sensor in the front will help to detect any obstacle.

8th

Alarming the user

The shoe will beep if any obstacle is present and will help to prevent the person from falling.

9th

Notifying care taker

If a person falls and he his wearing shoe then the gyroscope sensor will detect the orientation of shoe and will give a signal to the care taker of that person.

10th

Saving the life

Real time notification will help to notify the care taker and the person can be uplifted in short span of time.
